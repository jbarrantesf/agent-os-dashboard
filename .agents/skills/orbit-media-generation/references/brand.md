# NexAI Solutions CR — Brand Guidelines

## Visual Identity

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Dark Blue (#0D1B2A)
- **Secondary**: Cyan/Neon (#00D9FF)
- **Accent**: Lime Green (#00FF41)
- **Background**: Off-white (#F5F5F5)
- **Text**: Charcoal (#2C3E50)

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Modern, bold, tech-forward (Montserrat/Inter)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (Open Sans/SF Pro)
- **Monospace**: Code examples (JetBrains Mono)

### Photography Style
- **Aesthetic**: Minimalist, high-contrast, well-lit
- **Subjects**: Modern offices, professionals, technology, Costa Rica landscapes
- **Mood**: Innovative, trustworthy, forward-thinking
- **Lighting**: Natural light, clean shadows, bright environments

### Logo & Branding
- **Logo**: "Orbit" (🪐) — circular, modern, tech-inspired
- **Tagline**: "AI that scales. Without burning your time."
- **Voice**: Professional but approachable, technical but clear

## Use Cases & Examples

### Website Hero Image
- Modern Costa Rican tech office
- Team collaborating on AI solutions
- Bright, natural lighting
- 1920×1080 minimum

### Product Marketing
- AI agent dashboard interface
- Real-time data visualization
- Metrics and insights display

### Social Media
- Short-form content, snappy visuals
- Brand colors prominent
- Clear messaging
- Mobile-first design

### Educational Content
- Step-by-step AI process visualization
- Clean infographics
- Professional motion graphics
- Easy to follow

## Prompting Tips for Image Generation

**Include these when generating marketing materials:**
- "NexAI Solutions CR brand aesthetic"
- "Modern, professional, tech-forward"
- "Clean white space, high-quality photography"
- "Dark blue and cyan color accents"
- "Costa Rica tech company vibe"

**Avoid:**
- Overly stylized or cartoonish aesthetics
- Dark, moody lighting
- Cluttered compositions
- Generic corporate imagery
